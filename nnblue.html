Lifelong researcher. Asking the questions others won't since 2012.

Alex Saunders — known as Nugget — is the founder and host of Nugget's News. An ex-pharmacist who quit in 2017, Alex had been actively speaking out about the failings of the financial and banking system since 2012, before launching the YouTube channel to take it public.

His focus has always been the same: sound money, health freedom, world news, privacy, self-sufficiency, and sovereignty. Helping people wake up to the reality of the world we live in, understand who's really running it, and reclaim their own sovereignty.

"Trying to help people become the best version of themselves — wake up to what's really going on, who's really running the world, and reclaim our sovereignty."

Nugget's News has published more than 1,000 videos and reached over 14 million total views. Along the way, Alex has had more than 100 videos removed from YouTube — without a single explanation email from the platform.

That's the point. When they start removing it, you know it matters.

FAQ

Got questions?
Here are the honest answers.

What is Escape The Matrix?+
Escape The Matrix is the Nugget's News private membership platform. Your subscription gives you access to a growing course library, a private global community (through the member portal and private Facebook group), and one-on-one calls with Alex and the team.
How is this different from the free YouTube channel?+
The YouTube channel is where we publish our reporting — the news, interviews, and analysis. Escape The Matrix is the practitioner's layer: structured courses, a focused community, and direct access to bring your specific questions.
What topics are covered in the courses?+
OpSec and digital security, privacy tools, Bitcoin and sound money, privacy-focused cryptocurrencies, decentralised finance and applications, self-hosted AI, off-grid living, health and medical freedom, and world news and political corruption.
Do I need to be technical to use this?+
Not at all. That's exactly what Escape The Matrix is here for. Every course is built from first principles — no prior knowledge assumed. Whether you've never heard of a VPN or don't know the difference between Bitcoin and a blockchain, the courses walk you through everything step by step. By the end, you'll be the one people ask.
What does the one-on-one call include?+
Every membership includes access to scheduled one-on-one calls with Alex and the team. Bring your specific questions — privacy setup, financial situation, course content, or anything else. Use the member portal to book your session.
Is this just about crypto?+
No. Crypto and sound money are one category among several. Escape The Matrix covers digital privacy, decentralised AI, off-grid living, health freedom, and world news and political corruption. Crypto is a tool for financial sovereignty — one piece of a much bigger picture.
